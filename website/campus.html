A rising star among green enclave, Rathnavel Subramaniam College takes a wide range of initiatives to sustain the natural salubrious eco-centric atmosphere. The campus, hedged by a diverse dense flora offers a pleasant atmosphere with spacious, cozy, and well-ventilated classrooms. We shape our students towards holistic excellence. Over the last two decades, several new Programmes have been introduced and the institution has emerged as a trusted destination for quality higher education and as a hub for research and innovation. Students of our College have the opportunity to choose the course of their study according to their choice enabled with Choice Based Credit system in par with Academic Bank Credit system. The College prides itself as a significant institution in adapting its learning delivery platform which caters the current industrial needs. This has resulted in creating a vibrant ambience in campus that engages students in a variety of ways

Our college has a dedicated Internet Leased line of 200 Mbps, connected throughout the Campus. Students and faculties are free to access internet after the regular working hours. This helps the students to prepare papers on the latest technologies to be presented in various symposiums and seminars. (24 hours Internet facility is provided) The Intranet allows faculty to post lectures, notes and presentation documents on the college e- portal.
